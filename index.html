Abstract

Power electronics play a crucial role in improving the effectiveness and efficiency of energy storage systems (ESS) and the charging process for electric vehicles (EV). As the automotive industry rapidly adopts electric technology, there is a growing demand for reliable and scalable solutions for EV charging and energy storage. To meet the changing demands of the electric mobility ecosystem, this project focuses on the integration of power electronics, which is a key component. The main objective is to examine the design, implementation, and efficiency of power electronics systems in order to seamlessly integrate EV charging and ESS components. By doing so, the project aims to create an adaptable and high-performance infrastructure that can meet the varying energy requirements of EVs, while also improving the compatibility with the power grid and utilizing renewable energy sources.
